About Me

Hi, I’m Niranjan Epili—a passionate video editor with a flair for visual storytelling. With 4 years of experience, I’ve honed my skills in software like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve. My approach to editing blends creativity with precision, ensuring each frame contributes to a compelling narrative. Whether it’s a corporate promo, a music video, or a documentary, I thrive on transforming raw footage into captivating visual journeys. Let’s collaborate and bring your ideas to life!
